Which columns would influence the response variable the most?¶

1: History.

The history column describes a persons credit history, and how well they have managed their loans and debt in the past, and this would be a good predictor of how likely someone might be to repay any future loans given out by the bank.

2: Employment

The employment column tells us how long someone has been employed, and the longer someone has been employed, the more likely it is that they make more money, thus it is more likely that they have the financial resources available to repay a new loan.

3: Job

The job column tells us what kind of job a person has, from low/unskilled labor to highly skilled labor, and this again ties in to employment, and if someone is working a low or unskilled job, they might not make as much money, and therefore might not be able to pay back the loan, and vice versa for those in higher skilled positions.
